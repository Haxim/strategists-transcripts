Shannon Phillips

host

Shannon Phillips is a former Alberta cabinet minister and long-time political leader with extensive experience in government and opposition. She served as Minister of Environment and Parks, playing a central role in climate policy, energy debates, and intergovernmental negotiations.

On The Strategists, Shannon brings firsthand experience of governing — including the constraints, compromises, and pressures that shape political decisions. Her perspective grounds discussions in the realities of public office and offers insight into how policy and politics collide once campaigns end.
