Annalise Klingbeil

host

Annalise Klingbeil is a communications professional and political advisor with experience in public affairs, strategic communications, and issue management. She has worked in and around Canadian politics, helping organizations navigate complex policy environments and public scrutiny.

On The Strategists, Annalise brings a communications-first lens to political strategy, focusing on how messages land, how narratives form, and where political actors succeed or fail in connecting with the public. Her analysis emphasizes tone, framing, and the real-world impact of political communication.
