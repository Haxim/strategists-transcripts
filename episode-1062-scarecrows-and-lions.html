The Strategists

Episode 1062: Scarecrows and Lions

May 15, 2023

The gang take stock of the Alberta election and unpack the polls, the events, and everything from the effect of Mother's Day to whether it's now socially unacceptable to support the conservatives in swaths of Calgary to how the UCP might regain momentum going into debate week. Plus, Annalise farms the Discord and Twitter for questions, including: why are political leaders so eager to break out the windbreaker in an emergency?

Corey Hogan and Stephen Carter discuss the second week in the Alberta election: the state of the race, the looming debate, and how campaigns act when they’re halfway between takeoff and landing - plus, inexplicably, audience questions. Is Danielle Smith’s campaign drowning in her past comments? Should the NDP concerned about the “Shy Tory effect”? And that’s all great, but is Bret(t) spelled with one “T” or two? Zain Velji, as always, isn't here. But Annalise Klingbeil is, and she's got you covered.
