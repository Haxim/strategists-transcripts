May 17, 2022

The gang decides it's time to dig into the Ontario election and Stephen Carter decides its time to pick fights with Ontario political podcasts. Topics include: message discipline (or lack thereof), controlling your brand (or failing to do so), debate moderation (and the ignoring of the same) as well as storytelling in politics and why sometimes - usually, in fact - the biggest tactical mistake you can make is hating your opponent. PLUS: how to make everything about housing.

Are the Liberals and NDP strategically misfiring? And what does it reveal about Stephen that he was so quick to assume David Herle was talking about him? Zain Velji, as always, picks the questions and keeps everybody in line.
