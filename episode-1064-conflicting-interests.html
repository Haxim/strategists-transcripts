Episode 1064: Conflicting interests

May 19, 2023

It's debate night in Alberta. The gang assembles to unpack and process their thoughts in real time. They discuss the lines that worked, the strategies, the overall impressions and how each side can "win the debate" - all against the backdrop of an ethics violation that gives the entire affair a surreal energy.

Corey Hogan and Stephen Carter discuss the leaders’ debate in the 2023 Alberta election and an ethics commissioner ruling that hangs over it all. Did either leader do what they needed to do to be declared a “winner”? Will Danielle Smith be able to buffalo her way out of consequences for a damning ethics ruling? And who doesn’t love a blue blazer? Zain Velji, as always, isn't here. But Annalise Klingbeil is, and she's got you covered.
