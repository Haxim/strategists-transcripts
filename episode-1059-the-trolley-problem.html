Episode 1059: The Trolley Problem

May 8, 2023

The gang discuss the first week of the Alberta election campaign - one that started with a tax pledge and ended with a wildfire derailing business-as-usual - and unpack what campaigns are looking for seven days in.

Corey Hogan and Stephen Carter discuss the first week in the Alberta election and what campaigns assess seven days in, before turning to the Alberta wildfire situation, Danielle Smith making a troubling comparison and a new “contender” in the provincial election. Was Rachel Notley right to offer her support to Danielle Smith for dealing with the Alberta wildfires? When does a party know it’s all over? And what other words can we add to our CTRL-F list? Zain Velji, as always, isn't here. But Annalise Klingbeil is, and she's got you covered.
