The gang discuss the first week of the Alberta election campaign - one that started with a tax pledge and ended with a wildfire derailing business-as-usual - and unpack what campaigns are looking for seven days in.

Was Rachel Notley right to offer her support to Danielle Smith for dealing with the Alberta wildfires? When does a party know it’s all over? And what other words can we add to our CTRL-F list? Zain Velji, as always, isn't here. But Annalise Klingbeil is, and she's got you covered.
