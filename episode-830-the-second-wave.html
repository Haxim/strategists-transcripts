Episode 830: The second wave

November 16, 2020

Corey Hogan and Stephen Carter talk about how the federal government and provinces are responding to spikes in Covid-19 numbers and rate recent political (and "apolitical") developments in Alberta. Is the federal government doing everything it can? Will the Alberta Auditor General's report hurt the UCP government? And are three guys who gave themselves the name "The Strategists" really in a position pass judgment on anybody else's level arrogance? Zain Velji, as always, picks the questions and keeps everybody in line.
