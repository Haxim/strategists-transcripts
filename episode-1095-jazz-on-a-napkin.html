Stephen Carter rode a mountain bike and at his age is regretting it. Zain Velji and Annalise Klingbeil showed up to have the tables turned on them. The gang discusses the role of media - journalists and commentators - when it comes to platforming views and the changing nature of journalism.

Zain Velji and Annalise Klingbeil unpack the strategy of hosting, tackling issues like building rundowns and executing transitions before a deep dive into the changing nature of journalism and a host's obligations when interviewing controversial guests. Where is the line between responsible journalism and irresponsible platforming? Is journalism evolving or dying? And why wasn't Vivek invited to do the podcast? Corey Hogan turns the microphone on the two hosts and Stephen Carter isn't here.
