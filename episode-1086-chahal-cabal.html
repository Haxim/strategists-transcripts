Episode 1086: Chahal Cabal

July 31, 2023

Annalise is at Folk Fest. Zain Velji is decidedly not. Stephen and Corey answer questions around the Calgary Stampede, George Chahal, Liberals that have won Ontario elections and Liberals unhappy with their federal lot. Plus: the gang create a whole bunch of new merchandise.

And when will the debate between winning and losing be resolved? Zain Velji, as always, picks the questions and keeps everybody in line.
