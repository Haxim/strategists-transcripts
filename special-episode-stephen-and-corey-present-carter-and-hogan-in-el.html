PATREON EXCLUSIVE. Stephen Carter and Corey Hogan go deep into the troubles at Twitter, stepping in their time machine to go back to the heady day that was October 25 to give advice to soon-to-be-Twitter CEO Elon Musk. They use their powers of strategy and hindsight to build a plan that will remake Twitter in Elon's image - in a fashion that looks at least 5% competent.
