2023-04-28

The gang talk go through a grab-bag of political issues that include A.I. generated ads, the muted response by opposition leaders on the VW battery plant and the CBC president's letter to Pierre Poilievre before diving into the ongoing public sector strike.

Corey Hogan and Stephen Carter perform a “strategy check-in” on the CBC, A.I. in politics and more before turning to the ongoing PSAC strike in the federal public service. Are Justin Trudeau and Doug Ford in a full-blown “bromance”? Can PSAC keep the public on their side during a prolonged strike? And where can we find the audio files Stephen Carter is creating of Zain and Corey? Zain Velji, as always, picks the questions and keeps everybody in line.
