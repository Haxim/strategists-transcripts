September 3, 2024

The gang dive deep into Kevin Falcon suspending the BC United campaign and the fallout - for Falcon, for supporters of the now defunct BC United, for those who will wear this collapse, for the BC NDP and more.

Is the name change to blame? How will the BC NDP need to adjust their campaign? And now that the podcast wars are unilaterally settled, can we expect the gang to sweep the next award season? Zain Velji, as always, picks the questions and keeps everybody in line.
